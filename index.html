How it works

Intersignal runs client‑side. We collect visible text, compute integrity diagnostics, and — when available — call the browser’s local summarizer. No logins. No tracking. No server round‑trips.

  • Right‑click launcher + toolbar popup
  • Clean, readable UI with status and error banners
  • Privacy‑first: stays on your device

Current build: v0.6.1. Chrome’s built‑in summarizer is rolling out in waves; features auto‑enable when your browser exposes window.ai.

FAQ

Why isn’t the summary appearing yet?

Chrome’s local summarizer (Gemini Nano) is being rolled out gradually. The extension UI works today; summaries automatically activate when your Chrome enables the Summarization API. Until then you’ll see a polite banner and full integrity diagnostics.

Do you track pages I analyze?

No. Intersignal runs locally and does not phone home.

How do I launch it?

Use the toolbar button or right‑click a page and choose Intersignal: Summarize + Score This Page.
